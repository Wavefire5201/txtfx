## What are Masks?

Masks let you control which regions of the image effects apply to. Paint foreground and background regions, then set each effect to target one or the other.

## Painting

- **Foreground brush** (`B`) — paint the foreground region (green tint)
- **Background brush** (`N`) — paint the background region
- **Brush size** — adjust with `[` and `]` keys or the slider
- **Feather** — softness of the brush edge

## Effect Regions

Each effect has a "Region" selector:
- **Background** — effect only appears in unpainted areas (default)
- **Foreground** — effect only appears in painted areas
- **Both** — effect appears everywhere

## Tips

- Toggle mask visibility with `M` to see what you've painted
- Use a large, feathered brush for soft transitions
- Undo/redo works for mask strokes (Cmd/Ctrl+Z)
- "Clear mask" in the tool panel removes all painting
