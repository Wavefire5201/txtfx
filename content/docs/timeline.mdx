title: Timeline
description: Control playback and effect timing
---

## Playback Controls

- **Play/Pause** — Space bar or the play button
- **Skip to start** — rewinds and pauses
- **Skip to end** — jumps to end and pauses
- **Duration** — controls the loop length (default 10s)

## Effect Bars

Each effect has a colored bar on the timeline showing when it's active. You can drag the start and end handles to adjust timing.

## Effect Modes

- **Continuous** — effect loops within its timeline range
- **One-shot** — effect plays once and stops (good for typewriter, decode)

## Scrubbing

Click anywhere on the timeline ruler to jump to that time. Drag while paused to scrub through the animation frame by frame.
